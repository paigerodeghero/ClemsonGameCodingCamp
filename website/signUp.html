Computer Game Coding Camp for Autism is a camp for high school students on the autism spectrum (ASD) that exposes them to high-level video game development and networking with peers. While developing their video game, students will improve their teamwork skills such as communication and collaboration. Throughout the two-week camp, campers learn the high-level processes of software development, game development, interpersonal communication, collaboration, and end up with their own game, from scratch.

We are excited to host this camp for the third year in a row. This year, our coding camp will only be provided remotely.
