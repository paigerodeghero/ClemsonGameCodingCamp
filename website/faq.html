Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What are the dates of the camp?
    • 2022 Camp: July 11th - July 29th (M-F)
  2. When is the deadline for registering for the 2022 camp?
    • Early Registration Deadline: March 15, 2022
    • Late Registration Deadline: April 30, 2022
  3. What equipment is required to complete the camp?
    • A computer (see question 5 for spec information)
    • A video camera (built into computer or one that can be connected to the computer)
    • A microphone (built into computer or one that can be connected to the computer)
    • Paper
    • Pen and Pencil
  4. What software is required to complete the camp?
    • Godot game engine (Standard Version)
    • Zoom
    • Discord
  5. What computer specs are required?
    • Computers should be able to run Zoom and GoDot at the same time.
    • iPhones, iPads, and Chromebooks will not work for this camp.
    • Any computer made in or after 2018 should suffice.
    • If you are looking for a computer, Clemson has recommendations for student computers: CCIT Computer Recommendations
    • If you have any questions about your computer, please do not hesitant to reach out to discuss your machine.
  6. Is there homework?
    • We do provide optional homework throughout the camp. However, if the homework is not completed, that is okay and the camper will not fall behind.
  7. What will my child learn?
    • We will cover game design, game development (programming), collaboration skills, and team work skills. We will also have campers practice these skills throughout the camp.
    • Check out the camp schedule on our Sign Up page!

Please email us with any additional questions you may have!
